Viewing your child's prescription through the NHS App

Prescription view is safe, secure and thorough. It will allow you to view your child's prescriptions through linked profiles on the NHS App.

This new service has been developed in response to the increasing popularity of online health services.

Before applying for prescription view

You can access prescription view through linked profiles on the NHS App.

Linked profiles on the NHS app allows someone other than the patient to access and manage parts of their GP online services account.

People who sign up for linked profiles are usually responsible adults, such as parents or carers.

Before you can sign up to prescription view, you must be:

  • the child’s biological mothers

  • able to prove your relationship with the child (for example, with a birth certificate)

  • living with the child at the same address

  • registered at the same GP surgery

  • registered on NHS login and NHS App

Your child must be 10 or under before you sign up for prescription view.

If you have more than one child under 10, you can apply for prescription view for them, too.

Getting set up for prescription view

Once you are registered on NHS login and the NHS App, you will be asked: 

  • about potential safeguarding issues

  • to share your personal information

  • about your relationship to the child

  • about what you want to do on behalf of the child

  • your child’s details

You will be given a reference number to confirm that your application is complete.

After you have been approved for prescription view, you can switch to your child’s linked profile after logging in to the NHS App.

Benefits of using prescription view

This new service allows you to: 

  • view your child’s prescriptions on the NHS App or website

  • switch between your NHS profile and your child’s linked profile seamlessly

  • save time when it comes to managing your child’s healthcare tasks

In future the service will allow you to:

  • self-serve, reducing contact time with your GP surgery

  • manage your child’s healthcare tasks at a time that works for you

We look forward to onboarding you onto prescription view.
